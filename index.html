Nina's 2021 Book List

Hey! This is Nina :) Reading books is my 2021 new year resolution and I read 12 books in total, which includes different genres.

And for each book, I included the book name, author and a short introduction. Hope this can give you some inspirations when you want to find something to read.

About Rating

Some authors shared their own life stories or impressive momments they saw. Others created a wonderful imaginery world, impressing me with their creativity. However, there are also books that did not touch me as much as others do.

One Star

  • Sadly, these books are not my type.
  • Books that are not so interesting and usually took me a long time to finish.

Two Stars

  • These books are okay!
  • I still want to finish them when I am free. Feel the drive to continue reading. Recommended!

Three Stars

  • They are fantastic!
  • Books that I prefer to stay overnight to read. Never regret reading these books. Strongly recommended!!
